Checker Backdrop Material

The Checker Backdrop is a simple material in black and white. It's supposed to be used for preview images and other primitives.

Inputs

Object Info 🢒 Color and Alpha

Shader's base color and transparency are influenced by object's Viewport Display Color.

Outputs

Cycles and EEVEE BSDF

Streamlined material output that favours speed over quality.

Fallback Principled BSDF

For different render engines and GLTF export.

Unfortunately, Object Info is not implemented in Hydra Storm, resulting in a completely transparent material.
