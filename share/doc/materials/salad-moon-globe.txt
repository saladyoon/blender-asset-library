This is an emissive globe made out of "canvas" as a part of "Salad Moon Globe"
collection.

Outputs

- Cycles and EEVEE BSDF
    Streamlined material output that favours quality over speed.

- Fallback Principled BSDF
    For different render engines, GLTF export and textured preview.

    Unfortunately, Object Info is not implemented in Hydra Storm, resulting in
    a completely transparent material.
