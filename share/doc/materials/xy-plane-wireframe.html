XY Plane Wireframe Material

The XY Plane Wireframe is a simple material in yellow color. It's supposed to be used for preview images and other primitives.

Its colors are based on Catppuccin Machiatto palette.

Outputs

Cycles and EEVEE BSDF

Streamlined material output that favours speed over quality.

Fallback Principled BSDF

For different render engines, GLTF export and textured preview.
