Simple Diffuser Material

The Simple Diffuser is a transmissive material, used for simple props that provide light. It applies a fixed roughness value that mimics light diffusers, which are commonly seen in lighting equipment.

Inputs

Object Info 🢒 Color and Alpha

Shader's base color and transparency are influenced by object's Viewport Display Color.

Outputs

Cycles and EEVEE BSDF

Streamlined material output that favours speed over quality.
