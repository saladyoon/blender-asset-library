Generic Wireframe Material

The Generic Wireframe is a simple bright-ish material. It's supposed to be used for preview images and other primitives.

Its colors are based on Cattpuccin Machiatto palette.

Outputs

Cycles and EEVEE BSDF

Streamlined material output that favours speed over quality.

Fallback Principled BSDF

For different render engines, GLTF export and textured preview.
